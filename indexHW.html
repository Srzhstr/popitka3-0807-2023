Philip Emeagwali

Philip Emeagwali is a Nigerian computer scinetist . He has been living in the United States for many years.[1] He won the 1989 Gordon Bell Prize ($1,000) for price-performance in high-performance computing applications, in an oil reservoir modeling calculation using a novel mathematical formulation and implementation.[2][3]
